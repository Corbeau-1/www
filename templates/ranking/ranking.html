Ranking

Rulesof the Ranking List

  • Playershave to complete at least two games to be displayed in the rankinglist.
  • Playerswho have not played in any of the last 10 games are not displayedbut their scores are stored in the database and will be displayed oncethey play a new game.

RankingAlgorithm

  • Eachnew LT player is assigned a score of 1000.
  • Losersof an LT game lose P% of their score.
  • Winnersof an LT game equally share the total points lost by the losing players.So, winning with fewer players brings more points to the winners encouragingsmaller alliances/teams.
  • Thevalue of P is constant (10%) for team and no-diplomacy (lone wolf) gamesregardless of the team size or number of teams/players as every team/playerhas an equal chance to win.
  • Foralliance games, P is larger/smaller for a smaller/larger winning allianceand is calculated as follows: 
    Q=T/W=(total number of players) / (number of winners):
ifQ <= 2 then P = 0%
if2 < Q <= 2.5 then P = 2.5%
if2.5 < Q <= 3.5 then P = 5%
if3.5 < Q <= 4.5 then P = 7.5%
if4.5 < Q <= 6 then P = 10%
if6 < Q <= 10 then P = 12.5%
ifQ >10 then P = 15%
    • Soif a smaller alliance wins, losing players lose more points and winners sharemore points among fewer players (a double advantage).
