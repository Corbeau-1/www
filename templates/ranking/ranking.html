Longturn Ranking

Rules of the Ranking List

  • Players have to complete at least two games to be displayed in the ranking list.
  • Players who have not played in any of the last 10 games are not displayed, but their scores are stored in the database and will be displayed once they play a new game.

Ranking Algorithm

  • Each new LT player is assigned a score of 1000.
  • Losers of an LT game lose P% of their score.
  • Winners of an LT game equally share the total points lost by the losing players. So, winning with fewer players brings more points to the winners encouraging smaller alliances/teams.
  • The value of P is constant (10%) for team and no-diplomacy (lone wolf) games regardless of the team size or number of teams/players as every team/player has an equal chance to win.
  • For alliance games, P is larger/smaller for a smaller/larger winning alliance and is calculated as follows: 
    Q=T/W=(total number of players) / (number of winners):
ifQ <= 2 then P = 0%
if2 < Q <= 2.5 then P = 2.5%
if2.5 < Q <= 3.5 then P = 5%
if3.5 < Q <= 4.5 then P = 7.5%
if4.5 < Q <= 6 then P = 10%
if6 < Q <= 10 then P = 12.5%
ifQ >10 then P = 15%
    • So if a smaller alliance wins, losing players lose more points and winners share more points among fewer players (a double advantage).
