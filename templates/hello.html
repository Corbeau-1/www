Welcome to Longturn

You are here, because you enjoy Civilization-like games, and wish to play a multiplayer game with 20-30 human players, across a couple of months. You should get started by reading the introduction and rules. If you like what you see, create and account and sign on to a game. If you're lucky, an experimental game is running and you can be spawned right away!
